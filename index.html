Lucius Kwok's iOS Skills

I am a full stack iOS app developer and this page introduces some of the skills that I have which are needed to bring an app from an idea to a fully executed item in the App Store. This is an overview of the skills that are covered in the Skills Detail page:

  • Starting from an idea and using sketches and non-code prototypes to flesh out the idea and the UX design.
  • Conducting market and user research to find out what is out there.
  • Going from wire frames and storyboards to the first working prototype of the app in code.
  • Doing user testing and refining the prototype into a minimum viable product.
  • Managing the project and the people, to incorporate different people's work (art, audio, etc.), and to keep track of bugs and other issues.
  • Marketing, including creating the text and images to use on both the App Store description page and for a website, to let people know about the app.
  • Post-launch activities including bug fixing and providing user support.
