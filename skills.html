iOS Skills Details

Here is a detailed outline of the skills that I have for making iOS apps:

  • Ideation and Research
    • Making sketches and prototypes to capture the app idea
    • User research to find out what problems people actually need solving
    • Market research to find out what apps are already out there
  • Wire framing and Storyboarding
    • Refining sketches into specific screens and views
    • Creating the overall flow from screen to screen
  • Coding and Frameworks
    • Programming languages: Swift, Objective-C, C++
    • Language features such as closures (blocks), generics, and memory management such as retain-release and ARC.
    • Interface Builder and Storyboards
  • UI/UX Design
    • Layout and thematic design
    • Color scheme and theming
  • Artwork and Graphic Design
    • App icon and logo design
    • Button images and custom control element design
  • Audio and Sound Design
    • Sounds for UI feedback, notifications, and alerts
    • Music selection, if necessary
  • Testing and Debugging
    • User testing, including observational studies and getting feedback
    • Unit testing and other programmatic means of finding bugs
    • Testing on a variety of device types, screen sizes, and OS versions
  • Project Management and Issue Tracking
    • Version control, including managing repositories and managing branches
    • Issue tracking, including bug tracking and testing to confirm that an issue has been resolved
    • Maintaining the project schedule and managing the scope of the project, including preventing scope creep and decision making with regard to features
    • Compliance with App Store rules, including code signing
  • Marketing and Website Development
    • Creating and maintaining the website for the product
    • Adding and updating the App Store SKU for the app
    • Providing customer support
